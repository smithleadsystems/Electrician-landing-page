Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Smith Lead Systems uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website smithleadsystems.co.uk.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure our website functions correctly
  • Understand how visitors use our site (analytics)
  • Remember your preferences
  • Track the effectiveness of our marketing

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled. Session
Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g. Google Analytics). Up to 2 years
Marketing Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertising. Up to 1 year
Functional Remember your preferences and settings. Up to 1 year

4. Third Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third party services we use, including:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • GoHighLevel — CRM and form tracking
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Facebook/Meta — social media and advertising

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies which we encourage you to review.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out — install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.

7. Contact Us
